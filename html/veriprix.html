Overview

Background

The cost of the same medication varies among different pharmacies.

Problem

Insurance costs become convoluted due to intermediaries such as Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBM) and wholesalers.

Solution

Provide accurate and transparent drug pricing using data analytics.

Background

The cost of the same medication can vary depending on the pharmacy.

Price variations between pharmacies can range from 170% to 330%.

When patients are unable to access their medications or view the costs for each one, it leads to dissatisfaction and poor adherence.

The Harms of Opaque Pricing

Increased Cost

Compromises treatment decisions

Higher expenditures

Problem

  1. Insurance pricing is clandestine and convoluted due to intermediaries such as Pharmacy Benefits Manager (PBM) and Wholesaler.
  2. No platform shows insured drug pricing while 89% of the U.S. population has insurance.

Solution

Insurance Company
All data will be obtained from data brokers and insurance companies and directed to a proprietary databank.
Proprietary Databank & Data Analysis
This approach eliminates the need for secretive pricing information from PBMs and wholesalers, thereby reducing intermediaries in the prescription drug market.
Accurate Drug Price Prediction
Accurate drug pricing can be predicted through data analysis, making this information accessible to both physicians and patients.
Transparent Price
VeriPrix empowers insured patients with reliable and transparent pricing information at local pharmacies.

Final Product

Personalized Drug List

Patients can save their frequently used medications and view a list of prices and pharmacies on the main screen simultaneously.

Insurance-Based Pricing

Patients can enter their insurance information to accurately display the differences in medication coverage for each plan.

Easy Compare & Accurate Information

Patients can quickly compare medication prices across different pharmacies and view the exact location and details of each pharmacy.
